Gammy & The Living Things Synopsis

“An agama lizard finds the perfect excuse to exchange the boring and slow-paced life of the Hinterland for the fast-paced and success-paved streets of Lagos. With his band of music-loving friends, they set out on an arduous but event-filled adventure to make it big and conquer Lagos. Will they find success or redemption…“

Meet The Characters

NG

An overlooked female lizard member of the colony. She encourages Gammy to live up to his responsibility of leading the colony.

OGA

Oga is a chameleon that takes on different personalities as needed.

GAMMY

An agama Lizard who finds the routine of colony life at Hinterland boring. He dreams of and embarks on a journey outside the colony, performing with his band.

Dr.KOKO

Dr. Koko is an Inventor and a Biologist at the University of Hinterland.

Red Eye

Red Eyes is a gang leader interested in protecting his terrain

WU

The ever wise and patient mentor who has helped tutor the species of The Hinterland Basin for several years.

Fante

A member of Gammy’s band, Fante is a friendly, saxophone-playing Toad who comes from the “overseas”.

Endy

Endy, a female gecko, is Gammy’s friend who often serves as the voice of his conscience.
